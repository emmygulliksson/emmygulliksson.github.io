About me

I am a 25 years old positive and driven girl born in Örnsköldsvik, Sweden. In 2018, I moved to Umeå to study the Master of Science program in Interaction & Design, where I was allowed to combine my technical interests and creativity to create and design various applications.

In 2022, I had the opportunity to do an exchange semester at La Tobe University in Melbourne, Australia. That experience not only broadened my professional skills but also contributed significantly to my personal growth.

I graduated in 2023, equipped with versatile knowledge in programming, design, user testing, project management, and behavioural science.

If you want to know more, don't hesitate to contact me!
